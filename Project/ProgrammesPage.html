Our Programmes

Explore the courses and modules we offer.

Diploma in Computer Science (DCS)

A 2.5-year programme designed to build a strong foundation in programming, networking, system development, and emerging technologies.

Semesters Offered:

  • Semester 1 – Fundamentals of Computing
  • Semester 2 – Programming & Logic
  • Semester 3 – Database, Web & Networks
  • Semester 4 – System Development & Java
  • Semester 5 – Security, IoT & Capstone
  • Semester 6 – Industrial Training

Diploma in Business (DIB)

A practical business programme focusing on accounting, marketing, management, and entrepreneurship.

Core Modules:

  • Business Communication
  • Marketing Principles
  • Financial Accounting
  • Business Law
  • Business Mathematics

Diploma in Airline Services (DAS)

A specialized programme preparing students for the aviation and airline service industry.

Core Modules:

  • Airport Operations
  • Customer Service
  • Safety & Emergency Procedures
  • Airline Hospitality
