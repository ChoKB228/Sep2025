Short Story:The Ginderbread Man



Once upon a time, an old woman baked a gingerbread man. When she opened the oven, the gingerbread man jumped out and ran away, shouting, "Run, run, as fast as you can! You can't catch me, I'm the Gingerbread Man!"

The woman and her husband chased him, but he was too quick. Soon, a cow tried to catch him, then a horse, and even some farmers-but none could catch the Gingerbread Man.

At last, the Ginderbread Man reached a river. He couldn't swim, then a crocodile offered him a hand. "Hop on my back... I'll carry you across the river..." said the crocodile. The Gingerbread Man hop on the crocodile back. "Ha! Ha! Ha! You will never catch me I'm the Gingerbread Man"said The Ginderbread Man.

But then, halfway across the river.The crocodile tossed the Gingerbread Man upwards and with a single "CHOMP!". The Gingerbread Man was no more.
>

Moral:Be cautious about who you trust, especially strangers who seem too helpful.
